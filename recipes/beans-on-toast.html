Beans on toast

Description

The ultimate speedy dinner. (Or lunch, or breakfast...)

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • Tin of baked beans
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the beans just like it says on the tin
  3. Spoon over hot buttered toast and sprinkle with plenty of grated cheese
  4. Make sure your dinner is fully cooked and piping hot before serving it up
